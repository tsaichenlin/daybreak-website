WHO ARE WE?

DAYBREAK IS A SANTA CLARA UNIVERSITY BASED KPOP COVER DANCE CREW AND CLUB FOUNDED IN 2019. IT IS A GROWING COMMUNITY THAT BRINGS TOGETHER PEOPLE WITH THE SHARED LOVE FOR KPOP AND DANCE.

OUR MISSION

OUR MISSION, AS DAYBREAK, IS TO BUILD A DIVERSE COMMUNITY CENTERED AROUND THE APPRECIATION AND LOVE FOR KPOP THROUGH DANCE. AS A CREW, WE LOOK FORWARD TO PROMOTING A POSITIVE ENVIRONMENT WHERE WE WILL CULTIVATE TEAMWORK, SELF-CONFIDENCE, AND PASSION FOR DANCE.

WHAT WE DO

DAYBREAK HOST DANCE WORKSHOPS, SOCIAL EVENTS, AND END-OF-YEAR SHOWCASE. OUR DANCE CREW FILMS COVERS AND PERFORMS AT VARIOUS EVENTS.
